Our Story

How we fell in love

Spring 2019

The First Hello

It was a rainy Tuesday at the coffee shop on 5th Avenue. Sarah was reading a book about architecture, and Michael accidentally spilled his latte reaching for a napkin. What started as an embarrassing moment turned into a three-hour conversation about dreams, cities, and the perfect cup of coffee.

Summer 2020

Distance Makes the Heart

When the world shut down, we discovered that love transcends distance. Nightly video calls, handwritten letters, and surprise doorstep deliveries kept our connection strong. We learned that we could weather any storm together.

Autumn 2022

Adventures Together

From hiking mountains to exploring hidden bookstores, from cooking disasters to perfect pasta nights—we created a lifetime of memories in just a few years. Every adventure was better because we were together.

Winter 2024

The Question

On a snowy evening in December, Michael took Sarah back to that same coffee shop where it all began. With nervous hands and a racing heart, he asked the question he'd been dreaming of. Through happy tears, she said yes.
